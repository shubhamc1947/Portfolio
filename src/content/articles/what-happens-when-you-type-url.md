You type `www.google.com` into your browser and hit Enter. Within milliseconds, a fully rendered page appears - but what actually happened in between?

This article traces every step of that journey: DNS resolution, TCP handshakes, TLS negotiation, HTTP requests, server-side rendering, and finally the browser's paint cycle. Understanding this full-stack story makes you a sharper engineer whether you work on the frontend, backend, or anywhere in between.
